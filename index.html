About Relish

A bakery counter, a breakfast table, and a juice press — under one roof.

Tucked just off Birnin Kebbi Crescent, opposite the housing estate's 1st Gate, Relish Food opens its ovens early and doesn't slow down until 10PM. It's the kind of place where you can grab a loaf on your way to work, sit down for a full breakfast plate with the family, or stop by for a chilled juice on a hot Abuja afternoon.

Casual, easy-going, and genuinely good for kids and groups — pull up a chair, or call ahead and we'll have it ready to go.
